How the diagnostic runs — The Mitoro Method

01

Intake & Scoping

A short questionnaire on your current pain points, structure, and spend — followed by a written scope confirmation. What's in and out is explicit, on paper, before anything starts.

Days 1–2
02

Diagnostic Review

Review of whatever materials you provide, plus structured conversations with you and key staff. I map your current workflows and cost centers as they actually run — not as the org chart says they run.

Days 3–8
03

Analysis & Prioritization

The top 3–5 structural or cost issues, each with an estimated financial impact, ranked by effort-to-impact — so you know exactly what to fix first.

Days 9–11
04

Delivery

A written report plus a walkthrough call. A clear end-of-scope statement — any further work is proposed separately, never assumed.

Days 12–14
